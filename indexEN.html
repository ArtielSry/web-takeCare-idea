How it works

Make them feel safe.

TakeCare provides a wristband that allows the wearer to find out where he/she is via the TakeCare App.

This product is aimed at people who want to receive this assistance.

Whether you are near or far.

You won't have to worry when you have to be away.

Keeping privacy.

Operates only when the sensor is activated.

Take it with you.

Download the app that will help you to know if everything is well.

Respect their space.

It is important that each person feels respected in their space and therefore the location of the wearer is not indicated if the wearer does not wish to be shown.

IncIt includes only three buttons for ease of use:

Drop button

It will send a message to the nearest hospital or an SMS to the emergency number set up on the wristband.

Localization button

It will send a message both to the person wearing the wristband and to the emergency number indicating the exact address where the person is located.

Quick call

Pressing the call icon will contact the person who is configured as the emergency number.
